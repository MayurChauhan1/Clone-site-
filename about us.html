About Us

Our Story

Harish and Smitha's Inspiring Tale: How Acre 5 Became a Sanctuary for the Soul

Harish and Smitha, once city dwellers from Bengaluru, longed for a tranquil escape from urban chaos. Their search led them to Chikmagalur, where they established Acre 5—a coffee estate transformed into a sanctuary for the soul. With unwavering dedication, they cultivated acres of coffee, inviting nature’s beauty to thrive. Peacocks danced, morning mists embraced the estate, and Acre 5’s monsoon rains rejuvenated the land. Today, Acre 5 is a testament to their belief in nature’s power to heal and rejuvenate.

VISION

Nature's Elegance, Your Escape

Our vision is to offer an escape where guests immerse themselves in nature's elegance, finding respite from urban life.

MISSION

At Acre 5, our mission is to blend nature’s grace with exceptional hospitality, allowing guests to rejuvenate amidst Chikmagalur’s beauty. We aim for sustainability while delivering a home-like atmosphere.
